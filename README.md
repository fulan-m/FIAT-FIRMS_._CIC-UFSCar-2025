  # About
  This study was presented at the _"Congresso de Iniciação Científica – CIC, o Congresso de Iniciação em Desenvolvimento Tecnológico e Inovação – CIDTI e o Congresso de Iniciação Científica do Ensino Médio - CIC-EM"_ at the home institution of the Federal Universty of São Carlos (UFSCar) during October 21st and 22nd 2025.

  Available here are a few scripts used for the analysis of hotspot occurences in the Cerrado biome of São Paulo state using the VIIRS S-NPP to assess wildfire hotspots during the period of 2012-2024.
